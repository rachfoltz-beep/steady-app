                Backup & restore

                Data is saved in this browser (it survives closing the tab). To keep a copy elsewhere—iCloud, Google Drive, email, or another computer—download a backup file. Restoring replaces all Steady data in this browser with the file’s contents.

                Starting fresh is still progress

                Relapse is common in recovery. What matters is what you do next. If you’d like, set a new start time — your check-ins, journal, and habits stay here unless you clear them in the browser.

                If you’re in crisis or need support, reach out to a trusted person or local helpline. This app is not medical advice.
